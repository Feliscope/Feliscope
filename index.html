Our Comprehensive Services

We offer specialized expertise across the entire spectrum of computational biology, from fundamental omics analysis to cutting-edge molecular design and machine learning applications.

Omics analyses and functional interpretation

  • Best practice for Omics, including data preprocess, differential analyses, genetic variant calling, etc.
  • Causal pathogenic variant/biomarker screening for research/clinical projects.
  • Genetic regulatory mechanisms exploration using GWAS, QTL, mendelian randomization, etc.
  • Multi-Omics integration approches: DIABLO, autoencoder integrating with deep learning structures.
  • Functional analyses including pathway analyses, network anlayses, etc.
