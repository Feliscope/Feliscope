About Feliscope

Feliscope is a computational biology and AI consulting company dedicated to translating big data into biological insights. Our team provides end-to-end analytical solutions—from raw data processing to publication-ready reports—helping researchers, biotech startups, and pharmaceutical companies accelerate discovery and innovation.

Yuhang Zhang, Ph.D.

Founder & Principal Scientist

As the Founder and Principal Scientist of Feliscope LLC, I transform complex biological data into actionable insights that drive biomedical innovation. With a Ph.D. in computational cancer immunology and over six years of experience in multi-omics and AI modeling, I integrate genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics through machine learning frameworks to uncover biomarkers and disease mechanisms.

My academic experience at Harvard focused on large-scale clinical cohort analysis, single-cell and spatial omics, and RNA-based therapeutic modeling. Through Feliscope, I deliver scalable bioinformatics solutions that bridge data science and biology.

Services

Omics Analysis & Functional Interpretation

  • Omics preprocessing, DGE, genetic variant calling
  • Pathogenic variant/biomarker identification
  • Genetic regulatory mechanism exploration (GWAS, QTL, MR)
  • Multi-Omics integration
  • Pathway & network analyses

Molecular Design & Optimization

  • Design and optimize antibodies, siRNAs, ASOs
  • Molecular docking and interaction simulations
  • Stability, binding affinity, off-target evaluation

AI & Machine Learning Applications

  • Translate biological questions into AI-compatible formats
  • Modeling with scikit-learn, PyTorch, TensorFlow, Scanpy
  • Interpret model results for biomarker prioritization

Pipeline Development & Reproducibility

  • Standardized pipelines: AWS, Docker, Snakemake, Nextflow
  • Automate variant calling, RNA-seq/proteomics, single-cell analyses

Publication-Ready Visualization & Reporting

  • High-quality figures in R (ggplot2, Shiny) & Python (matplotlib, seaborn, plotly)
  • Produce reports with network diagrams, pathway maps, heatmaps, plots
